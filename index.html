What This Service Does

Missed Call Assistant is an automated missed-call notification service. When a customer calls a participating business and no one answers, the system sends a single SMS to the caller confirming their message was received and that the business will return their call. The system also sends a summary notification to the business owner.

Data We Collect

When a call is processed, the system may collect and store the following information:

  • Caller's phone number
  • Date and time of the call
  • Voicemail audio recording and transcription
  • Vehicle or service information mentioned by the caller

How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to:

  • Send the caller a single automated confirmation text message
  • Notify the business owner of the missed call and its contents
  • Log the call in the business's private call record

No data is used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to the missed-call notification described above.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. Call data is shared only with the specific business whose phone number you called, for the purpose of returning your call.

We use third-party services (Twilio for SMS delivery, Deepgram for transcription, OpenAI for message parsing) solely to operate the service. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from using it for their own purposes.

Data Retention

Call records are retained for operational purposes and may be deleted upon request. To request deletion, contact us at the email below.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving automated messages from this service at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no further automated messages will be sent to your number.
